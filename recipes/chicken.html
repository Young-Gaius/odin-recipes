SPICY CHICKEN

Description

Indulge in the fiery flavors of a spicy chicken meal that will tantalize your taste buds. This mouthwatering dish features tender chicken pieces marinated in a bold blend of aromatic spices. The chicken is cooked to juicy perfection, allowing the spices to penetrate every bite. With each mouthful, you'll experience a delightful combination of intense heat, smokiness, and a hint of tanginess. This versatile dish can be paired with fluffy steamed rice or buttery naan bread, providing a cooling contrast to the spice. Prepare to embark on a flavorful journey that will leave you craving for more of this irresistible spicy chicken meal.

Ingredirnts

  • Chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Onion powder
  • Tomato paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the chili powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic, ginger, salt, black pepper, cumin, coriander, turmeric, onion powder, tomato paste, and lemon juice to create a marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them thoroughly. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or refrigerate it overnight for more intense flavors.
  3. Preheat your grill, stovetop pan, or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. If grilling, preheat the grill and lightly oil the grates. Place the marinated chicken onto the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has reached an internal temperature of 165Â°F (74Â°C).
  5. If using a stovetop pan, heat a bit of cooking oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook for about 4-6 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. If using an oven, preheat it to 400Â°F (200Â°C). Place the marinated chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil. Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the heat source and let it rest for a few minutes.
